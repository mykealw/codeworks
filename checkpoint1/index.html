Blink-182

ABOUT BLINK-182

Blink-182 is an American rock band formed in Poway, California, in 1992. Their current lineup consists of Matt Skiba, Mark Hoppus, and Travis Barker. Though their sound has diversified throughout their career, their musical style blends catchy pop melodies with fast-paced punk rock. Their lyrics center on relationships, adolescent frustration, and maturity-- or lack thereof.

Album Information

Blink-182 has sold over 13 million albums in the United States, and over 50 million albums worldwide. Their full discography include 8 studio albums, 1 demo album, 1 live-recording album, and a greatest hits album. Their best selling album is Enema of the State, which reached #9 on the Billboard top 200. Their second best selling album, Take Off Your Pants and Jacket reached #1.
